The Unofficial Patreon Button

Showcase your Patreon project with this lightweight badge. Display your project's total pledge amount, its number of patrons, or both.

  • Shows real-time pledge amount and/or number of patrons
  • Links to your Patreon project
  • Two available sizes
  • Use the hosted version from ptrn.me: no cost, no ads, no tracking, HTTP & HTTPS!
  • Can be self-hosted (fork at Github and go to town with it)
